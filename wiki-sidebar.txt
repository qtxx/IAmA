# Submitting:

## AMAs should be about:
* Something uncommon that plays a central role in your life, or
* A truly interesting and unique event.
* [Explanation and examples of this rule can be found here](http://goo.gl/PJa2j)

## All AMAs require proof.
* Proof should be included in the text of the post when you start your AMA. If it must remain confidential, you can [message it to the moderators](http://goo.gl/QPM7x) and we can verify you.
* [See here for tips concerning proof and examples](http://goo.gl/TtNhm)

## Request threads
* Requests must be reasonable and realistic. All requests must have 5 questions for the person being requested.
* **Requests for celebrities must contain their public contact info in the body of the request.**
* [See here for information about requests](http://goo.gl/duMoL)

# Commenting:

## Please note:
* All initial responses to posters must contain a properly punctuated question.
* [See more on our comment removals policy here](http://goo.gl/sm9MHh)
